## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Melody Insights ‚Äì Music Streaming Behavior & Performance Dashboard

**Domain:** Music Streaming Analytics / Business Intelligence / Data Visualization

**Tools Used:** Power BI Desktop, DAX Measures, Power Query (data transformation)

**Dataset Details**  
- 1,000 song/stream records  
- Key fields: User Subscription (Free/Premium), Streams Count, Genre (Pop, Rock, Hip-Hop, Electronic, etc.), Artist, Device Type (Mobile, Desktop, Tablet), Stream Date, Avg Duration, etc.  
- Total Streams: 255,072 | Total Users: 1,000 | Total Songs: 1,000

**Business Objectives**  
- Understand **subscription impact** on streaming behavior (Free vs Premium)  
- Identify **genre popularity**, device preferences, and artist dominance  
- Analyze **temporal trends** in global streaming activity  
- Provide actionable insights for music platforms, artists, and marketers (e.g., target Premium users, promote high-engagement genres)

**Key Dashboards**

1. **User Behavior & Subscription Insights**  
   - Free vs Premium Streaming Comparison: Free users generate higher total streams (~120K+) vs Premium (~120K)  
   - Users Split: 518 Free | 482 Premium  
   - Device Usage: Mobile dominant (31.8%), Desktop (32.9%), Tablet (35.3%)  
   - Avg Streams by Genre & Subscription: Premium users stream more in Classical, Pop, Rock; Free higher in Electronic, Hip-Hop  
   - Insights: Mobile-first audience; Premium users show deeper genre engagement

2. **Global Music Streaming Performance**  
   - Top 10 Artists by Streams: Taylor Swift, Imagine Dragons, Arijit Singh, Adele, Ed Sheeran, BTS, Bad Bunny leading  
   - Genre Popularity Distribution (Donut Chart): Balanced across Pop (~29.4%), Rock (~30.3%), Electronic (~28.7%), etc.  
   - Streams Over Time (Line Chart): Monthly fluctuations with peaks in mid-year  
   - Avg Song Duration: ~4.039 minutes  
   - Top N Artist Filter (slicer for dynamic top 10)  
   - Insights: Pop/Rock dominate globally; Consistent streaming volume year-round with seasonal boosts

**Major Insights Delivered**  
- Premium users engage more deeply per genre ‚Üí opportunity for upselling features  
- Mobile is the primary device ‚Äî optimize for mobile listening experience  
- Taylor Swift & global stars (Arijit Singh, BTS, Bad Bunny) drive massive streams ‚Üí cross-promotion potential  
- Near-even genre distribution ‚Üí diverse catalog performs well  
- Stable monthly streams ‚Üí platform has loyal, consistent user base

**Skills Demonstrated**  
- Data cleaning & modeling in Power Query  
- Advanced DAX (rankings, averages by category, time intelligence)  
- Interactive visuals: Bar charts, Pie/Donut, Line trends, Slicers & filters  
- Dashboard layout, themes, tooltips & cross-highlighting  
- Storytelling with music industry-relevant metrics

## How to Explore the Project
1. Download the `.pbix` file(s) from the repository  
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop** (free)  
3. Use slicers to filter by:  
   - Subscription Type  
   - Genre  
   - Device Type  
   - Artist / Top N  
   - Date range  
4. Hover over visuals for detailed tooltips
